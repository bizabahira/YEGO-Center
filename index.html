Youth Empowerment Center

Empowering Youth for Opportunities

YEGO Center provides Free comprehensive programs designed to equip youth with essential skills for success in today's dynamic world. From vocational training, IT skills, and Talent development to entrepreneurship skills, civic education, and talent nurturing, we prepare young people to excel in their personal and professional lives. Join us to develop Skills, Network, creativity, and resilience for a brighter future.

Youth Empowerment Center

Empowering Youth for Opportunities

YEGO Center provides comprehensive programs designed to equip youth with essential skills for success in today's dynamic world. From vocational training, IT skills, and sports development to entrepreneurship, civic education, and talent nurturing, we prepare young people to excel in their personal and professional lives. Join us to develop leadership, creativity, and resilience for a brighter future.

Explore Programs

Vocational Training

Hands-on training in practical fields like carpentry, tailoring, and welding to prepare youth for diverse careers and self-employment.

IT Skills Development

Comprehensive IT training programs covering computer basics, software development, and digital literacy to empower future innovators.

Sports Programs

Engaging sports activities promoting teamwork, discipline, and physical fitness to nurture healthy and active lifestyles.

Entrepreneurship Training

Learn to build and manage successful businesses through workshops on innovation, financial literacy, and market strategies.

Arts and Crafts

Encouraging creativity through painting, design, and sculpture workshops that inspire artistic expression and innovation.

Leadership and Civic Education

Programs focused on fostering leadership, responsibility, and community engagement for a brighter and united future.

Our Vision

Our Mission

Empowering Youth for a Better Future

Promoting decent youth employment using an integrated approach. We aim to mobilize, build capacity, and advocate for youth initiatives that lead to economic and social development as well as to a productive and patriotic Rwandan youth generation.
